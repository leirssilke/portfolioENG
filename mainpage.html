A summary


I remember drawing from the moment I could hold a pencil. I even recall painting with a brush for the very first time, and thinking Da Vinci would be proud of me and my big, red dot on the white paper. I was blisfully born in the year of 1996 and filled my time with reading and drawing, and getting stuck in my imagination. That's all I really cared for. I've had my share of life, I can assure you, and decided early on  that if I was to do something with my time on this earth, it'd be drawing

So now I'm a student at Sint-Lucas in the wonderful city of Ghent, currently in my third bachelor of illustration. Here you'll find many of my sketches that are a preparation for the “finished” works and lots of other things – like tattoo designs.

Have a look around, enjoy, and contact me in case you have questions. 
Send a mail to silke.leirs@gmail.com and I'll make sure to respond as soon as possible!

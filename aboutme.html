? Who am I

No seriously, let's talk about that!
I was born on 21/08/1996 in Belgium. I studied Health Sciences in high school for six years in the “technical” departement. Truth be told, those six years were utter hell. I found solace in the only thing that got me through those hard times: drawing. In the weekend I went to the Academy of Arts in my hometown Dendermonde, and soon began to realise I wanted to learn more.
When I graduated I went to LUCA School of Arts, did my entree exam and got accepted. I wanted to enter the Illustration course – because books had been a big part of my daily life, and I wanted to be able to make them myself. 
So here I am in my third year, and I'm planning to do a Master in the Arts. The more you learn the better, right? I still have my whole life to work, but let's agree that this is a good start.
